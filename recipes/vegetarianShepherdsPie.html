Vegetarian Shepherd's Pie

Description

A mix of vegetables cooked in soy sauce with a potato, soured cream, and cheese topping.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 red pepper (chopped)
  • 2 celery sticks (sliced)
  • 2 carrots (chopped)
  • 1 courgette (sliced)
  • Broccoli (chopped into sprigs)
  • 1 small pot of sour cream 3 oz cheddar cheese (grated)
  • Soy sauce (a good shake)
  • ½ teaspoon chilli flakes
  • Potatoes for topping Parmesan or cheddar (grated)

Steps

  1. Fry onion and red pepper gently till soft
  2. Meanwhile cook chopped carrot and celery in a saucepan with a little stock. Put lid on and simmer till not hard. Add broccoli sprigs and courgettes at end and cook till tender but not soggy.
  3. Add chilli flakes and soy sauce to onion and pepper mix and when absorbed add the carrots, celery, courgette and broccoli and stir together.
  4. Put veg in an ovenproof dish and top with grated cheese followed by sour cream – spread over in dollops.
  5. Top with mashed potato and grate a bit of parmesan/cheddar cheese on top.
  6. Put in oven and cook at Gas 175 till topping is brown and veg bubbling a bit.
